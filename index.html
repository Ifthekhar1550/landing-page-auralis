HOW IT WORK

Generate your product photo in seconds.

Just upload your product, select a scene, and watch our AI instantly create stunning visuals for your brand, ads, or social posts.

01

Upload your product photo

Take a clean photo of your product using your phone or camera on any background. Upload it to the tool, and our AI will detect and isolate the product automatically.

02

Choose a style or scene

Select from a library of backgrounds minimalist studio, lifestyle, seasonal, or custom brand scenes. You can adjust lighting, angles, shadows, and even add props to match your aesthetic.

03

Generate and download

In seconds, our AI creates high-resolution images with your selected style. Preview, tweak if needed, and download your final shots ready to use on your store, social media, or ad campaigns.

FEATURES

Packed with smart features that save you time.

Background removal

Automatically removes the original background from your product photo with a single click no manual editing needed.

Scene templates

A variety of AI-powered scene templates, including studio, lifestyle, and seasonal styles, are available for selection.

High resolution output

Get high-resolution, crystal-clear product photos that are prepared for the web, print, or marketplaces.

Fast generation

Generate high-quality product images in just seconds no rendering delays or long processing times.

Bulk generation

Upload multiple product photos at once and generate scenes in bulk—a huge time saver for agencies and large shops.

Multiple Variations

Create several styled versions of the same product image to test different looks for ads, content, or marketplaces.
